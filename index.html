Speaker

After an early career as a university lecturer in Soil Science, Andrew Young was called into pastoral ministry and served first as the pastor of the Evangelical Presbyterian Church in Christchurch (1981-90), and then the Westminster Presbyterian Church of Indooroopilly, Brisbane, Australia (1991-94).

In 1995 he was asked to serve as founding Principal of Grace Theological College in Auckland. After 13 years in that role, Andrew returned briefly to pastoral ministry before his wife’s serious illness necessitated his retirement in order to care for her.

From 2012 until the present, Andrew has acted as the Director of the Ezra Ministry, a writing, research and teaching ministry committed to promoting the spiritual development of leaders. His passion is to help Christians know and experience God more deeply in their personal lives and ministries.

Talks

Where Worship Fits: What place does worship play in the life of a Christian? What is worship? These are critical questions to ask and answer today. (1 Peter 2:4-5)

The God we Worship: Worship is a response to God’s self-revelation. But what is God like? Our perception of God has an enormous influence on how we live and worship. (Exodus 20:3-6; 1 Thessalonians 1:9-10)

The Difference Jesus Makes: Jesus himself is the truest worshipper that has ever lived. What influence does his life and work make on the way we worship God? (Mark 1:35-39; Hebrews 10:19-22)

Private, Family and Corporate Worship: How does our understanding of worship influence these three traditionally accepted forms of worship? Are they still valid and important for us today? (Deuteronomy 6:4-9; Matthew 6:5-6; Psalm 22:22-24)
